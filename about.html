About

Compassionate RPN with 15+ years of comprehensive healthcare experience, specializing in geriatric and vulnerable population care. My unique integration of clinical expertise, health informatics proficiency, and cultural competence enables exceptional patient-centered care across acute, complex, and community healthcare settings.

My nursing journey began in my family's charitable hospital in Egypt, where fifteen years of volunteer work instilled the core values of compassionate care: that true healing combines clinical skill with genuine human connection and preserved dignity.

Throughout my Canadian clinical training across surgical, complex care, and acute settings, I've cultivated composure under pressure, clear therapeutic communication, and an unwavering focus on patient safety and comfort. Colleagues describe my approach as consistently calm, patient-centered, and optimistic.

Committed to lifelong learning, I continuously integrate new knowledge to enhance patient care, clinical leadership, and healthcare technology application.

I believe in lifelong learning and view education as a continuous journey. My goal is to learn something new every day and apply it to enhance patient care, leadership, and technology integration in healthcare.

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.” — Mahatma Gandhi

My life’s purpose is simple yet fulfilling: to help people, to bring comfort in difficult moments, and to put a smile on their faces — because those smiles are the true rewards of my profession.
